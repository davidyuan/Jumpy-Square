Game summary
....................
Jump off walls and dodge blocks in spectacular ways. 
It's not just about dodging the blocks, but how you do it.
- Just tap to jump, it's easy to learn but hard to master
- Earn medals for special maneuvers you do in the game
- Compare your score to others in the Leaderboard
- Show off your medal collection to friends
- Music composed by Domique Lufua himself
- Built exclusively for iOS
